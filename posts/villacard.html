From Idea to Launch: How I Built VillaCard

September 30, 2025 · 6 min read

Home Blog

VillaCard began as a weekend idea — a smarter booking experience for high-end villas, powered by AI. I wanted to combine the best parts of property management, concierge services, and digital design.

The Stack

  • Next.js 15 for the app structure
  • Supabase for authentication and data
  • AI Concierge logic built with local LLM APIs

Lessons Learned

- Simplicity wins — users value clean interfaces over flashy animations.
- AI should feel invisible — not gimmicky.
- Build the core experience first, then polish the edges.
